Privacy Policy

Saku · Erek Studio

Short version: Saku collects nothing. It has no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. It works fully offline, and everything you do stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Saku does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no sign-up, no profile, and no account.

Data stored on your device

Saku saves a few things locally so the app remembers your choices:

  • Your settings (theme, haptics, error-guard, accent palette).
  • Whether you have purchased the one-time Studio Unlock.

This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Network & third parties

Saku makes no network requests of its own and bundles no advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting SDKs. Puzzles are generated on your device.

Purchases

The optional Studio Unlock is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. Saku never sees your name, email, or payment details — those are handled entirely by Apple or Google under their own privacy policies.

Children

Saku is safe for all ages: it collects no data and shows no ads or external links inside gameplay.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and its effective date.
